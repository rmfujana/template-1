About Us

Your Trusted Partner for Feed Additives and Nutrition

PT Levotama Langgeng Sejahtera, established in 2016, is a company engaged in the trade and distribution of feed additives and feed supplements. We are committed to providing optimal nutritional solutions for the livestock feed industry in Indonesia. With a wide and robust distribution network, we ensure the timely availability of high-quality products across the nation. Additionally, we offer nutritional consulting services to assist farmers in achieving improved productivity and efficiency.
