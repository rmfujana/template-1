About Us

Your Trusted Partner for Feed Additives and Nutrition

PT Levotama Langgeng Sejahtera, established in 2016, specializes in the trade and distribution of feed additives and supplements. We are committed to providing optimal nutritional solutions for the livestock feed industry in Indonesia. Our extensive distribution network ensures timely delivery of high-quality products nationwide.
